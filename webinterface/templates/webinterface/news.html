CATAMI wins at the WAiTTA Incite Awards 2013-2014

CATAMI was recently awarded a Western Australian Information Technology and Telecommunications Alliance (WAiTTA) Incite Award under the Society Domain, Sustainability category (http://www.waitta.asn.au/). We are honoured to receive this award and would like to thank all the people who worked on the project including the marine ecology researchers who gave up their time. We are now nominated for the National iAwards in August.
