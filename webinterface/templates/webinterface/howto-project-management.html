Catami HowTo: Manage projects in CATAMI

Delete, export, import and bookmark projects

Deleting a Project

Ensure you have logged in and have the right permissions. Once logged in, select a project you wish to delete, and click on the red 'Delete' button located on the top left corner of the screen


The following confirmation dialog will appear, click on the confirmation button to proceed with deletion of project


Exporting a project

Ensure you have logged in and have the right permissions. Once logged in, select a project you wish to export, and click on the 'Download CSV' button located on the top left corner of the screen



The following confirmation dialog will appear, click on the confirmation button to export the project
