Catami HowTo: Upload data to CATAMI

Important! You'll need to be logged in to upload data to CATAMI.

1. Prepare your data

The Data Format

CATAMI expects data to be prepared according to the CATAMI Data Specification. You can find this on the CATAMI wiki .

For a typical image based deployment, this will consist of a directory of images and several descriptive text files.

Important! These text files have a specific format See more

With your data organised for CATAMI, you are ready to start uploading

What about video data?

CATAMI does not support video data at this time

2. Upload your data

Choose web-based or command-line upload

Web-based upload

The web-based upload tool is the preferred method of uploading data to CATAMI.

Command-line upload

The CATAMI command-line upload tool is written in Python 2.7 and has quite a few Python dependancies. The source code and instructions are available at the CATAMI github.
